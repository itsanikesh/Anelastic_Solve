Redraw

Ctrl-R is the keyboard shortcut for Redraw. By default, the right mouse button performs a redraw.

 

Tecplot does not automatically redraw the current frame after every change, unless you have selected the Auto Redraw check box on the sidebar.

 

Not selecting Auto Redraw will allow you to make many changes at once without having to wait for Tecplot to completely redraw the frame after each change.
