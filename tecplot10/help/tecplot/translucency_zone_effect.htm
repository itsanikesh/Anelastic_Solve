Translucency Zone Effect

To create a translucent zone, the Contour or Shade zone layer check boxes must be selected from the sidebar.

 

You control the translucency of a zone using the Surface Translucency options on the Effects Attributes dialog.

 

The Translucency zone effect check box applies only to zones. It does not apply to slices, streamtraces, or iso-surfaces. Translucency for those objects is controlled through their respective Attributes dialogs.

 

Plots with translucency cannot be printed. Translucency will appear only on your screen, or in exported bitmap images.
