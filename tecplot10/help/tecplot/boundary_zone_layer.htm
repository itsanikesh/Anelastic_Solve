Boundary Zone Layer

Select the Boundary check box to turn on the Boundary zone layer.

 

The Boundary zone layer controls plotting of zone boundaries for IJ- and IJK-ordered zones. They appear as lines at some or all of the edges of the zone. Blanking does not affect the boundaries of IJ-ordered zones, but IJK-blanking of IJK-ordered zones does cause the boundary to adjust to outline the blanked region. Finite-element zones do not have boundaries, although the Data menu contains an option for creating a zone that represents the boundary of the finite-element zone.
