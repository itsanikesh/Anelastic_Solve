About Tecplot

Tecplot was first announced in 1988. Since then, Tecplot has become the visualization tool of choice for thousands of engineers and scientists.

 

Tecplot, Inc. builds software for the visualization of scientific and engineering data and for the numerical simulation of fluid flows; and is active in research and application of its software for industry, NASA, and various branches of the U.S. Department of Defense.

 

Our objective is to provide high-quality engineering/scientific software tools and services to help engineers and scientists be more creative, efficient, and productive.
