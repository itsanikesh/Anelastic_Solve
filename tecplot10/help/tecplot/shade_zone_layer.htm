Shade Zone Layer

Select the Shade check box to turn on the Shade zone layer.

 

Light source shading adds a degree of realism to 3-D surface plots, particularly plots of physical objects such as machine parts. Shading can also help you discern the shape of the plot. Although most commonly used with 3-D surfaces, shade plots can also be used to flood 2-D plots with solid colors, or light source shade the exterior of 3-D volume plots.
