Mesh Zone Layer

Select the Mesh check box to turn on the Mesh zone layer.

 

Mesh plots are field plots of the lines connecting the data points within a zone.

 

For I-ordered data, the mesh is a simple line connecting all of the points in order.

 

For IJ-ordered data, it is a grid of lines.

 

For IJK-ordered data, mesh is either grids of lines on the Faces or on I-, J-, or K-planes, or a 3-D lattice of connections between points.

 

For finite-element zones, the mesh is a plot of all edges of all elements.

 

The mesh lines are straight lines between adjacent points.
