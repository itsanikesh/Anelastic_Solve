Insert Polyline

Select Insert Polyline to draw a polyline to any frame in your plot. Clicking in a frame creates a polyline connecting the points identified by the clicks.

 

To draw a polyline:

  1. Move the cursor into a frame.

  2. Click where you want to place the polyline starting point.

  3. Move the cursor and click at each desired break point.

 

To end the polyline either press Esc or choose a different mode from the sidebar Tools.
