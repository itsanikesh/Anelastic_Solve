Streamtrace Details Timing

Use the Timing page of the Streamtrace Details dialog to control attributes of streamtraces that apply to all streamtraces, those already placed as well as those about to be placed.

 

The Timing page controls timed markers for streamlines, and timed dashes for all types of streamtraces. Stream markers are drawn at time locations along streamlines. The spacing between stream markers is proportional to the magnitude of the local vector field. The spacing between markers is large in regions where the local magnitude is large, and small in regions of small magnitude. Streamtrace dashes are similar conceptually to drawing a streamtrace with a dashed line pattern. The streamtrace is "on" for a time interval, then "off" for another interval, then "on" again, and so forth.

 

Additional information is available in the Tecplot Users Manual.
