Delete Zone

Use the Delete Zone dialog to delete one or more zones in the current data set.

 

To delete a single zone:

1. Click on the zone in the scrolled list Select Zone(s).

2. Click Delete.

 

To delete a range of zones:

1. Click on the first or last zone in the range.

2. Shift-click on the last or first zone in the range.

3. Click Delete.

 

To delete a set of arbitrary zones:

1. Click on the first zone you want.

2. Ctrl-click on subsequent zones until you have selected all the zones in the set.

3. Click Delete.

 

You may not delete all zones in a data set. If you select all zones and click Delete, an error dialog appears informing you that you cannot delete the last zone, and zone 1 is not deleted.

 

Note: If your data set has only one zone, this dialog is not available.
