Scatter Legend

Use the Scatter Legend dialog to specify whether, and how, legends are displayed for the Scatter zone layer. The Scatter Legend does not appear unless scatter is turned on for a zone in the frame.

 

The scatter legend options fall into four categories:

  • Show: Specify whether the legend itself or the zone names are shown.

  • Text: Specify the color, font, and size of text in the legend.

  • Position: Specify the frame position and line spacing of the legend.

  • Legend Box: Specify the attributes of the legend box.
