Tools Probe At XY

Select the Probe At Tool to probe for values of the data set's variables at a particular point. As you move the cursor into the Tecplot workspace, the cursor changes to a cross hair. When you move into the axis grid area, the cursor cross hair is augmented by a vertical or horizontal line, depending on whether you are probing along the X-axis or the Y-axis. You can change the axis to probe simply by pressing X to probe the X-axis or Y to probe the Y-axis

 

To obtain interpolated values of the data set variables at the specified X or Y location:

  1. Click at the desired X- or Y-location.

  2. The Probe dialog appears with the requested information.

 

To obtain exact values for the data point nearest the cursor location:

  1. Ctrl-click at the desired location.

  2. The Probe dialog appears with the requested information.
