Probe At Position

Use the Probe At dialog to examine variable values or zone and cell information at any point along a specified axis. You can probe at either an axis position, or by cell indices. The Probe At dialog has two pages: Position and Index.

 

Position

  • Select Axis to Probe (Probe At Position only): Select the axis to probe from among the enabled options. By default, X1 and Y1 are enabled, and X1 is selected.

  • X or Y/R or Theta (Probe At Position only): Enter the X- or Y/R or Theta position at which to probe. Use the up or down buttons to increase or decrease the position.

  • Do Probe: Perform the probe operation, and display the results in the Probe dialog.

 

Index

  • Map (Probe At Index only): Select from the drop-down the map into which you want to probe. Then enter the I-, J- and K-indices of probe location. Use the buttons to increase or decrease the indices.

  • Do Probe: Perform the probe operation, and display the results in the Probe dialog.
