extract iso surfaces

Use the Extract Iso-Surfaces dialog to extract surfaces of equal value, that is, iso-surfaces, as zones. This dialog has no enterable fields or options; it simply informs you that it is changing your data by creating new zones and zone names. It gives you the chance to halt the process if you want keep the data from being altered, or to continue.

 

To proceed with the extraction, and creation of new zones, click Extract.

 

To stop the extraction, and keep the data unaltered, click Cancel.

 

Each iso-surface plotted in a 3-D volume zone is extracted as a new zone. The number of new zones equals the number of iso-surface levels. Note that each iso-surface zone may contain multiple topologically independent segments. The created zones are FE-surface, quadrilateral element-type zones, regardless of the source zone types. After generating the zones, you may plot them as any other FE-surface zone.
