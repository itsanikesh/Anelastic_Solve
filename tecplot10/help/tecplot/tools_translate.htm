Tools Translate

Change the viewing position of the data or paper (translate) or the view magnification of the data within the frame or the paper within the workspace.

 

While in Translate/Magnify mode, drag the cursor to move the data with respect to the frame, or Shift-drag to move the paper with respect to the workspace. You can rescale your image by pressing "+" to magnify, "-" to shrink. If you are Shift-dragging to move the paper, the rescale buttons "+" and "-" will magnify or shrink the paper so long as you have the mouse button depressed. When you release the mouse button, "+" and "-" revert to magnifying the data.
